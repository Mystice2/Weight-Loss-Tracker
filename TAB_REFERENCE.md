## Quick Tips

### For Best Experience:
- **Add to Home Screen** for full-screen app
- **Enable notifications** (if you add them later)
- **Log daily** at the same time each morning
- **Check Dashboard** weekly for trends
- **Use Plan tab** for meal inspiration

### One-Handed Use:
- Bottom tabs are thumb-friendly
- All important buttons are easy to reach
- Form inputs are large enough to tap
- Scrolling is smooth

### Data Management:
- Everything saves automatically
- No "Save" button needed except for logs
- Data persists even if you close the app
- Reset option in Settings if needed
